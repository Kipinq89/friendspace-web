Frequently Asked Questions
🤔 How do I customize my profile?
Navigate to the "Profile" or "Settings" page from the main menu. There you can change your display name, tagline, mood, profile photo, and even set your favorite profile song!
👥 How do I add friends?
You can add friends by clicking the "+ Add" button next to their name in the "People You May Know" section or by searching for them in the search bar and sending a friend request.
📷 How do I upload photos?
Go to the "Photos" tab and click on the upload zone, or simply drag and drop your images into the area. They will be added to your personal gallery.
💬 How do I send direct messages?
Click on the "Messages" tab in the navigation menu. Select a friend from your chat list on the left to start a real-time conversation.
🔒 How do I change my password?
You can update your password by visiting the "Settings" tab and selecting the "Security" section. Make sure to choose a strong password!

Still need help?
