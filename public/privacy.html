Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At FriendSpace, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, password, and profile details (such as your display name, mood, and tagline).
  • Content: Photos, posts, comments, and messages you create or share on the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with FriendSpace, including your IP address, browser type, and activity logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FriendSpace platform.
  • Personalize your experience (like showing you relevant friends and trending topics).
  • Communicate with you, including sending updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Ensure the safety and security of our community.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With other users, according to your privacy settings (e.g., your public profile and posts).
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Your Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Update your profile information and privacy settings at any time in the "Settings" page.
  • Request the deletion of your account by contacting support.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@friendspace.local.
